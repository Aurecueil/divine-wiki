### Audio Editing

<Tabs items={["Free"]}>
<Tab value="Free">

<ToolCard name="Quartz" href="/docs/tools/tex-thumbnail-provider">
  Has a `.wpk` editor / `.wem`.
</ToolCard>
<ToolCard name="LtMAO" href="/docs/tools/ltmao" badge="Recommended">
  Can convert `.wem` to `.wav` and back.
</ToolCard>
<ToolCard
  name="bnk-GUI"
  href="https://github.com/Morilli/bnk-extract-GUI/releases/tag/v1.5"
>
  Extracts and rebuilds `.wem` files from `.bnk` soundbanks.
</ToolCard>
<ToolCard name="Foobar2000" href="https://www.foobar2000.org/">
  LtMAO is better, but Foobar2000 also converts `.wem` files to `.wav` for audio
  editing.
</ToolCard>
<ToolCard name="Audacity" href="https://www.audacityteam.org/">
  Edits `.wav` audio files (cutting, layering, exporting).
</ToolCard>
<ToolCard
  name="Wwise 2019"
  href="https://www.audiokinetic.com/en/download/"
  badge="Advanced"
>
  Required to make custom `.bnk` files.
</ToolCard>

</Tab>
</Tabs>
