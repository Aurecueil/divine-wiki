### What You're Working With

League of Legends stores its audio in a few file types you'll encounter repeatedly:

- `.bnk` — a Wwise "SoundBank" that contains both audio data and/or logic/event hierarchy
- `.wpk` — a Riot-specific format that stores only raw audio (no metadata)
- `.wem` — the individual compressed audio format Wwise uses internally

<Callout type="info" title="Don't use custom skins in Korea or China">
  For **SFX** (abilities, impacts, UI sounds), you'll usually be editing an `audio.bnk` file.
  
  For **VO** (voice lines, announcer), you'll usually be editing an `audio.wpk` file.
</Callout>

### Finding the Right Files

You need three files to edit a character's sounds:

| File | Example path |
|---|---|
| Audio (wpk or bnk) | `ASSETS/Sounds/Wwise2016/VO/en_US/Characters/Yone/Skins/Base/Yone_Base_vo_audio.wpk` |
| Events bank | `ASSETS/Sounds/Wwise2016/VO/en_US/Characters/Yone/Skins/Base/Yone_Base_vo_events.bnk` |
| Bin file | `data/characters/Yone/skins/skin0.bin` |

You can load these files into Quartz directly or using one of pre-set options:

- **From game** — Just Select a champion and their skin (left most option).
- **From an existing mod** — If you're editing, sourcing or building on top of another mod, you can load the mod's folder instead (2nd left most option).

### Step-by-Step: Replacing a Sound

**1. Open the Sound Bank tab in Quartz.**

<img src="/wiki-images/quartz-empty-2.png" alt="" />


**2. Load your files on the LEFT side.**

Click the left panel and point Quartz to your `.bin`, audio (`.bnk` or `.wpk`), and events (`.bnk`) files. Then hit **Parse**.

This populates the left side with a list of all sounds in that bank.

<img src="/wiki-images/quartz-2-2.png" alt="" />


You can have multiple banks parsed and loaded on the left at the same time.

**3. Add your replacement sounds on the RIGHT side.**

The right panel is where you load the new audio you want to use. Quartz accepts:

- `.wav`
- `.wem`
- `.ogg`
- `.mp3`

It will handle format conversion automatically — no need to manually convert to `.wem` before importing.

You can also load another parsed League audio bank on the right side and pull sounds from it directly.

<img src="/wiki-images/quartz-3-2.png" alt="" />


**4. Drag to replace.**

Drag a sound from the **right panel** onto the sound you want to replace in the **left panel**. The entry on the left will update to reflect the replacement.

<img src="/wiki-images/quartz4.png" alt="Quartz Selection dot png" />

**5. Save your edited bank.**

Select your bank on the left panel, then click **"Save as BNK/WPK"** and save it somewhere in your mod folder.

<img src="/wiki-images/quartz-save-2.png" alt="" />


---

### What to Include in Your Mod

| Mod type | Files to include |
|---|---|
| SFX mod | `audio.bnk` only |
| VO mod | `audio.wpk` **AND** `events.bnk` |

For VO mods, the events bank is required because it defines which audio file plays for each in-game event (attack, death, joke, etc.). Without it, your new audio won't be triggered correctly.
